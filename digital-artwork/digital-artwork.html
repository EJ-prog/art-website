Serra Lives On

This piece is a digital image created in Adobe Illustrator. This was my Design 2 final project. It is inspired by the Magic the Gathering plainswalker: Serra. It includes the symbol for the mana type associated with Serra. Additionally, there are forget me nots with the intended meaning of rememberance.

The King of Comics

This piece was created in Adobe Illustrator and was my Design I final project. It was inspired by Jack Kirby, the comic book artist. It includes comics where he was the artist, a standing easel that he worked on, with a portrait of Jack Kirby on the easel. It also includes a drafting table setup basaed on one of Kirby's own setups. Featured front and center is the man featured on the Inkpot Awards, which Jack Kirby recieved in 1977.
